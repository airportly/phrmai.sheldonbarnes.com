Tools on the road to HumanOS.

Interactive pharma-AI tools — designed to communicate the science of biology, drug discovery, and the 3D genome through direct manipulation, not slides. Each tool is self-contained; together they accrete into a unified HumanOS.

by Sheldon Barnes · sheldonbarnes.com
Tool 01 · Chromatin

GenomeOS

Eight-level interactive tour of chromatin organization, from the whole nucleus down to individual atoms, anchored on the β-globin locus. Mitosis, loop extrusion, transcription, replication, and a narrated audio tour.

Vite React three.js Web Speech API
Tool 02 · Proteins

Molecule Viewer

Hands-free 3D viewer for Boltz-2 and OpenFold3 structure predictions. Ask the assistant to walk a protein chain, focus a residue, or launch a guided tour — powered by Mol* and browser speech recognition.

Mol* Voice control Static

Roadmap

PHRMAI is early. Each new tool is a self-contained folder in this repo that deploys as a static asset under phrmai.sheldonbarnes.com/<tool-name>/.

Planned additions include pathway browsers, single-cell explorers, drug-target interaction tours, and narrated walkthroughs that connect molecular events to clinical outcomes. Together they will form HumanOS — a unified, interactive mental model of human biology for pharma teams.
